C.0239(SJC)Loo lordynges here . þe lykenes of cryste The line numbering for MS C here jumps from line 166 to line 239. This is to account for the missing leaf containg HL lines 167-248. Although HL contains 82 lines in this section, I only skip 80 lines in numbering MS C, as this is the number of lines attested by MSS UDE through the same portion of the poem. Because MS C agrees strongly with UDE in HL lines 1-166 and 249-82 (see HL pp. lx-lxii), this seems the best guideline for numbering the missing lines from MS C.
